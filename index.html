SmartStep

The AI Navigation Assistant

What is SmartStep?

SmartStep was created to address the challenges faced by individuals with visual impairments in navigating their surroundings safely and independently. Traditional mobility aids like canes have limitations in detecting obstacles at various heights and distances. We envisioned a solution that integrates modern technology into everyday wear, offering enhanced spatial awareness and real-time assistance.

How It Works

SmartStep integrates a LiDAR sensor into the laces of a shoe, constantly scanning the environment for obstacles. Here's how the system functions:

  • Obstacle Detection: The LiDAR sensor measures distances by emitting light pulses and detecting reflections.
  • Alert Mechanism: When an obstacle is detected within a pre-set range, the system activates an alert. This can be in the form of vibrations or audio feedback, depending on the user's preference.
  • Customizable Feedback: Users can adjust sensitivity and feedback types through a companion mobile application, ensuring a personalized experience.

Key Features

  • Seamless Integration: Fits discreetly into any shoe design.
  • Real-Time Alerts: Provides instant notifications to prevent accidents.
  • Energy-Efficient: Designed to operate for long hours with minimal power consumption.
  • Expandable Ecosystem: Can connect with other smart devices for enhanced functionality.
